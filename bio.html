About Roxana Akhmetova

Roxana Akhmetova is a policy strategist and researcher specializing in AI governance, tech diplomacy, and automated decision-making systems in the public sector. She translates complex technology policy into actionable frameworks for governments, industry leaders, and civil society organizations.

Her doctoral research at the University of Oxford examines AI governance and regulatory oversight of automated systems in government deployments. Her work has directly informed policy: she was cited in the U.K. Government's 2024 Ethics Framework for Automated Decision-Making.

She is excited to contribute to mission-driven roles at the intersection of responsible AI innovation and policy implementation.
