Oxford AI Gala 2025

2025
Founded and chaired flagship AI governance conference, securing partnerships with Anthropic, Sony AI, RAND Europe, and Tony Blair Institute to convene 250+ senior stakeholders including C-suite executives, government officials, and academic leaders. Managed budget planning, sponsorship development, vendor coordination, and financial delivery, generating £50K revenue with £10K profit in inaugural year.

The Oxford AI Gala is a flagship AI governance convening, that brought together leaders in artificial intelligence policy, ethics, and technology. This AI gala featured industry partners including Anthropic, Sony AI, RAND Europe, and the Tony Blair Institute.

Held at the historic Oxford University Museum of Natural History, the Oxford AI Gala 2025 convened 250+ senior stakeholders including C-suite executives, government officials, and academic leaders to discuss the future of AI governance and responsible AI development.
